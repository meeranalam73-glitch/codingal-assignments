Why a Balance Meal Matters

Snacks can be healthy when we choose foods that give energy instead of only sugar. Fruits, nuts, sprouts, and yoghurt are better choices than chips and sugar drinks.

Nutrition Tips for Students

Drink Enough Water

Water helps the body stay active, supports digestion, and keeps the truth focused. Students should carry a water bottle and drink water throughout the day.

Eat Colorful Food

Different colours in fruits and vegetables give different nutrients.A colorful place often means a healthier plate.
